NexusForever: A WildStar Emulator is in the works!

Fans of the MMO Wildstar will be pleased to hear that an emulator, NexusForever, is in the works!

While its still in its early stages, developers have showcased a mostly functional housing system.

The project is up on Github if you want to check it out and contribute!
